## Priority Frameworks

### CLAS Standards (Cultural & Linguistic Appropriate Services)
**15 Standards in 3 Categories:**

1. **Governance & Leadership** (Standards 2-4)
2. **Communication & Language** (Standards 5-8)
3. **Engagement & Improvement** (Standards 9-15)

**Training:** Free at <https://thinkculturalhealth.hhs.gov/>

### Section 1557 (ACA Nondiscrimination)
**Protected:** Race, Color, National Origin, Sex, Age, Disability  
**Requires:** Language services, accessibility, grievance procedures

### WHO Health Equity Framework
**Key Publications (2025):**

- World Report on Social Determinants (May 2025)
- Life Course Approach Framework (July 2025)
- Disability Health Equity Initiative (June 2025)
