## Why It Matters
- Worker safety requirement; citations carry fines and increase liability

## Top 5 Actions
1. Maintain written Exposure Control Plan; review annually
2. Offer Hepatitis B vaccine; document declinations/acceptance
3. Implement safer sharps and no-recapping; evaluate devices annually
4. Train all at-risk staff initially and annually
5. Maintain sharps injury log and post-exposure protocols

## Red Flags
- Missing ECP, outdated training, sharps injuries trending upward
