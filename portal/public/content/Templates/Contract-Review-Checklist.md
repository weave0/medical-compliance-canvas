Last Updated: November 18, 2025

---

## Stark Exception Elements (as applicable)
- Written agreement signed by parties
- Term ≥ 1 year (if required)
- Compensation set in advance
- Fair Market Value (FMV) supported (attach analysis)
- Commercially reasonable irrespective of referrals
- Not determined by volume or value of referrals
- Scope and space/equipment (if applicable) are reasonable and necessary

---

## AKS Safe Harbor Elements (as applicable)
- Space/Equipment Rental: set terms, FMV, exclusive use limits
- Personal Services/Management: aggregate compensation set in advance, FMV, commercially reasonable
- Employment: bona fide employee, wages consistent with FMV
- Discounts/Warranties: documented, properly reflected in cost reports/claims
