# EMTALA Compliance Checklist

Last Updated: November 18, 2025

---

## Policies & Governance
- EMTALA policy defines MSE, stabilization, transfer, and QMP
- On-call policy with specialty coverage expectations and escalation
- Signage posted at ED/in-take areas (English + common languages)
- Transfer agreements and process flows maintained

## Staffing & Training
- QMP list current; scope compliant with state law and hospital bylaws
- Annual training for registration, triage, clinical staff, security
- Drills for transfer process and on-call escalation

## Documentation & Records
- MSE documented by QMP; time stamps for triage → MSE → disposition
- Stabilization treatments and re-evaluations captured
- Transfer forms complete (physician certification, receiving acceptance)
- On-call schedules retained (5+ years)

## Monitoring & Improvement
- Monthly review of transfers/high-risk cases
- Audit on-call response times and delays
- Corrective action plans assigned and tracked
